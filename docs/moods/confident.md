# Confident

"Confident" mood music projects assertiveness, self-possession, and control — bold production, strong vocal delivery, mid-tempo grooves (90-130 BPM), and lyrical content focused on success, identity, or determination. Energy is moderate to high; valence skews positive but not euphoric (the feeling is "I've got this," not "I'm overjoyed"). Genres heavily represented include hip-hop (especially confident-rap and braggadocio styles), pop, R&B, and certain rock subgenres.

Confident music is the soundtrack of self-presentation: getting ready to go out, walking into an interview, performing in any sense. It rewards listeners who want music that supports a steady, in-charge state rather than amplifying excitement or calming anxiety.
