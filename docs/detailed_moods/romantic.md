# Romantic (detailed mood)

A "romantic" detailed-mood tag signals music whose primary affect is intimacy and emotional warmth between two people — soft dynamics, melodic vocals (often duets or close harmony), tempos in the 65-95 BPM range that mirror slow dance, and lyrical content focused on attraction, devotion, longing, or shared memory. Production tends to favor warmth and intimacy over impact: close-mic'd vocals, acoustic instruments, soft electronic textures.

Heavy in R&B ballads, soul, soft jazz, certain pop, and bossa nova. Distinct from "happy" or "uplifting" because the emotional axis is interpersonal rather than internal — the song is about a relationship more than a state of mind. Listeners reach for romantic music for date contexts, shared quiet time, or solo daydreaming.
