# Uplifting

Uplifting music produces a sustained sense of hope, optimism, or quiet joy — major-key harmony, gradual dynamic builds, warm timbres, and lyrical content focused on perseverance, gratitude, or growth. Distinct from "happy" (immediate positivity) and "euphoric" (peak intensity) — uplifting is more durable and contemplative, often building over the course of a song rather than landing all at once.

Tempos run mid (90-130 BPM); energy moderate; valence high but with emotional weight. Common in indie pop, soul, gospel, certain electronic and post-rock styles. Listeners reach for uplifting music during recovery from difficulty, long-term effort, or moments needing emotional fuel — music to walk forward with rather than dance to.
