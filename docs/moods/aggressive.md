# Aggressive

"Aggressive" describes music that conveys hostility, defiance, or attack — distorted guitars, harsh vocals, fast-and-pounding drums, dissonant harmony, and lyrical content centered on anger, conflict, or rebellion. The mood overlaps heavily with metal (especially thrash, death, hardcore), punk, and aggressive hip-hop. Tempos run fast (140-220 BPM in metal and hardcore), with energy near maximum and danceability variable but typically secondary to impact.

Aggressive music is sought out specifically for emotional release rather than relaxation — to channel anger, frustration, or stress through controlled exposure to its sonic counterpart. It rewards listeners who use music as catharsis, and is unusually effective for high-intensity exercise where emotional intensity becomes physical fuel.
