# Synthwave

Synthwave is a retrofuturist electronic genre that draws explicit aesthetic and sonic inspiration from 1980s film soundtracks, video game music, and synth-pop. Production hallmarks: gated reverb drums, analog or analog-modeled synths, prominent arpeggios, sidechained basslines, and a glossy, slightly nostalgic atmosphere. Tempos sit 90-120 BPM with moderate-to-high energy and danceability.

Vocals are common but often secondary to instrumental atmosphere; many tracks are fully instrumental. Common moods are dreamy, cinematic, melancholic, or driving — frequently described as "running through a neon city at night." It rewards listeners who enjoy mood-piece electronic music with strong nostalgic or filmic associations.
