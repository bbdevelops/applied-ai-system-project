# Hip-Hop

Hip-hop is a vocal-forward genre built around rapped verses over sampled or programmed beats, with the rhythmic delivery (flow) of the MC functioning as a primary melodic instrument. Tempos cluster 70-100 BPM, with high danceability and moderate-to-high energy. Production styles range from boom-bap (hard drum samples, jazz/soul samples) to trap (rolling hi-hats, 808 sub-bass) to alternative hip-hop (live instrumentation, genre fusion).

Lyrically the genre is dense and specific — storytelling, social commentary, personal narrative, and wordplay — rewarding listeners who follow the lyrics closely. It scales emotionally from braggadocio and confidence to introspection and grief, depending on the artist's intent.
