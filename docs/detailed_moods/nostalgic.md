# Nostalgic (detailed mood)

The "nostalgic" detailed-mood tag is reserved for songs whose primary effect is triggered memory — recall of past places, relationships, or selves. Sonic markers commonly include older or imitation-vintage textures (analog tape warmth, period-specific synth presets, gated reverb drums, sample-based production), familiar chord movements, and lyrical content that explicitly references past time.

Nostalgia in music is more often bittersweet than purely sad — the warmth of remembering paired with awareness that the moment can't be revisited. Heavily represented in synthwave, soul, classic R&B, certain indie pop, and singer-songwriter work. Listeners reach for nostalgic songs to deliberately step into a remembered emotional state for a few minutes, knowing they'll come back.
