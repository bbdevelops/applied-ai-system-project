# Relaxed

"Relaxed" mood music supports physical and mental unwinding — low energy, slow tempos (60-90 BPM), warm acoustic or soft synthesizer textures, and uncomplicated melodic content. Valence trends slightly positive but not bright; the goal is steady calm rather than uplift. Heavy in folk, acoustic singer-songwriter, soft jazz, ambient, lofi, and downtempo electronica.

Distinct from "chill" in that relaxed leans more organic and less produced — more likely to feature acoustic guitar, piano, or upright bass than processed beats and synth pads. Listeners reach for relaxed music to ease out of high-stress states, for evening wind-downs, light reading, or recovery time. The mood is generous: it asks little and offers steady companionship.
