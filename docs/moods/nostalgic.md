# Nostalgic

Nostalgic music evokes recall of past time, place, or relationship — often through deliberate use of older sonic textures (analog tape, vintage synths, gated reverb), familiar chord progressions, and lyrical content that references memory, youth, or loss of what once was. Tempos vary; energy and valence trend moderate; the defining trait is sonic familiarity that triggers personal association.

Heavily represented in synthwave, certain indie pop, soul, and singer-songwriter work, but the mood can attach to any genre when the production cues memory. Listeners reach for nostalgic music to revisit emotional states tied to past time periods — bittersweet rather than purely sad, and often warmer in feeling than melancholic.
