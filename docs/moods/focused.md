# Focused

Music in the "focused" mood is built to support deep concentration without becoming distracting. Hallmarks: low-to-moderate energy, slow-to-mid tempo (70-110 BPM), high instrumentalness (minimal or no vocals), and repetitive, predictable structures that create a steady backdrop rather than commanding attention. Lofi, ambient, classical (especially Baroque and minimalist), and post-rock instrumental work dominate the category.

Focused music is the soundtrack of work — coding, writing, studying, deep reading — where lyrics or sudden dynamic shifts would pull attention away. It rewards listeners who want music as cognitive scaffolding: present enough to mask outside noise, predictable enough to disappear into the background of thought.
