# Intense

"Intense" music is high-energy and emotionally heavy — driving rhythms, loud dynamics, and a sense of forward pressure or urgency. Tempos typically run 120-180 BPM, with energy values pushed near maximum and danceability moderate to high. The category spans hard rock, metal, aggressive electronic music, and certain hip-hop subgenres — anything with sustained intensity rather than gentle build-and-release dynamics.

Intense music is the soundtrack of effortful focus: weight training, fast running, working under deadline pressure, channeling anger or determination. It rewards listeners who want music that matches and amplifies high internal arousal, not music that softens it. Distinct from "aggressive" in that intense doesn't require hostility — only force.
