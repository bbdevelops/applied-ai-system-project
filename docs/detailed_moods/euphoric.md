# Euphoric (detailed mood)

A "euphoric" detailed-mood tag marks songs that aim for peak emotional uplift through structural dynamics — long buildups, dramatic drops, anthemic choruses, layered counter-melodies, and moments of cathartic release. The hallmark is the sense that the song is *climbing* toward something, then delivering it. Tempos commonly 120-140 BPM; valence very high; energy maximum.

Distinct from a generally "happy" song because euphoric is a peak event, not a steady state. Heaviest in EDM, festival-format house, some pop, and arena-style alt-rock. Reach for euphoric music when you want the song to take you somewhere specific — over the threshold of a big chorus, a peak workout interval, or a moment that needs to feel larger than itself.
