# Chill

"Chill" describes music with low energy, moderate-to-positive valence, slow-to-mid tempos (60-100 BPM), and a deliberately unhurried, non-demanding feel. The category overlaps heavily with lofi, ambient, downtempo electronica, and acoustic singer-songwriter work. Production typically favors warm, soft textures — muted drums, mellow synths, fingerpicked guitar, electric piano — over bright, cutting timbres.

Chill music is the modern soundtrack of background time: studying, cooking dinner, riding public transit, easing into the evening. It rewards listeners who want music that creates a calm, low-stimulation atmosphere without demanding active engagement. Distinct from "relaxed" in that chill leans slightly cooler and more produced.
