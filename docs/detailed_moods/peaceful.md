# Peaceful (detailed mood)

A song carries a "peaceful" detailed-mood tag when its emotional center is steady, low-arousal calm — quiet warmth without melancholy, restfulness without sleepiness. Sonic markers: high acousticness or soft synth pads, very slow harmonic motion, sparse arrangement, no sharp transients, vocals (if any) intimate and unhurried. Energy is consistently low; valence is neutral-to-positive. Tempos hover 60-85 BPM.

Distinct from "chill" or "relaxed" because peaceful skews more contemplative — adjacent to meditation, prayer, or quiet ritual. Songs in this register reward patient listening and are usually chosen for transitional moments: morning awakening, evening quieting, recovery from stress. They aim to do less, more deliberately.
