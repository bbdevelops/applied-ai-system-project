# EDM

EDM (Electronic Dance Music) is an umbrella for synth-driven, beat-centric music designed for dancing and high-energy environments — house, techno, trance, dubstep, drum-and-bass, and their hybrids. Tempos cluster between 120 and 174 BPM depending on subgenre, with very high energy and danceability values, low acousticness, and structures built around long buildups and drops rather than verse-chorus form.

Vocals when present are often processed (vocoded, chopped, or pitch-shifted) and treated as another texture rather than a focus. Lyrical content is minimal — energy and emotional release are the message. It rewards listeners seeking propulsive, body-moving music: the gym, the dance floor, the long drive.
