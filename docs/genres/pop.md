# Pop

Pop is a broadly accessible style built around clear vocal hooks, polished production, and tightly arranged 3-4 minute song structures. Energy and valence are usually pushed high to deliver an upbeat, immediate emotional payoff: bright synths, programmed beats, layered backing vocals, and a chorus designed to land within the first thirty seconds. Tempo typically sits between 100 and 130 BPM, danceability is high, and lyrical content focuses on relatable everyday emotions — love, confidence, celebration, longing.

Modern pop borrows heavily from EDM, hip-hop, and R&B, blurring genre lines, but retains the same goal: maximum reach with minimum context required. It rewards listeners who want music that meets them halfway, immediately.
