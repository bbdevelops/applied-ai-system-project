# Rock

Rock is built on the electric guitar, drum kit, and bass guitar — typically loud, riff-driven, and centered on a strong vocal performance. Tempos commonly range 110-160 BPM, with energy values pushed high (0.7-0.95) and acousticness low. Subgenres span from melodic arena rock and indie rock through harder territory like punk, metal, and post-hardcore, but the unifying thread is the band-as-instrument and the prominence of distorted guitar.

Lyrically rock favors directness — frustration, defiance, longing, romance — delivered with intensity rather than subtlety. It rewards listeners who want guitar-forward music with momentum and emotional weight, whether the mood is aggressive, intense, or anthemic.
