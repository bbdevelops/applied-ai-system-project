# Uplifting (detailed mood)

The "uplifting" detailed-mood tag captures songs whose effect is sustained encouragement — a sense of forward motion, optimism, or quiet hope that builds and stays rather than peaking and dropping. Sonic markers: major-key or modal-major harmony, gradual dynamic builds, warm bright timbres, lyrics about resilience, perseverance, or recovery. Tempos commonly 95-130 BPM; energy moderate; valence high but with emotional weight.

Distinct from "happy" (immediate, surface-level positivity) and "euphoric" (peak intensity) — uplifting songs leave a trace, often used as personal anthems for difficult periods. Common in indie pop, soul, gospel, post-rock, and certain electronic and singer-songwriter work. Listeners reach for them when they need fuel to keep going, not just a mood lift.
