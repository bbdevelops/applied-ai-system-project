# Dreamy (detailed mood)

The "dreamy" detailed-mood tag signals music with a hazy, suspended, semi-detached emotional quality — long reverb tails, blurred timbres, slow harmonic drift, and vocals (when present) that float rather than declare. Tempos typically 70-100 BPM; energy low to moderate; valence ambiguous, leaning bittersweet rather than clearly positive or negative.

Common to dream-pop, shoegaze, ambient electronica, certain synthwave, and soft indie. The category prizes texture and atmosphere over rhythmic drive or melodic memorability. Listeners reach for dreamy songs during reflective time — late-night drives, watching rain, the fade between waking and sleep — anywhere reality benefits from softening at the edges.
